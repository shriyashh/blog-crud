Know About Us

Company Overview

Shriyash Software Solution Private Limited started with doing IT jobs for Film Industry, Entertainment industry, hotel industry software solution, custom software development, India Nationalized Banks, State Level Universities of Indian States and gradually entered into the automobile, higher education, hospitality and manufacturing sectors. It has developed numerous software in various segments and industries. TSS has the exposure on developing software from Basic level to latest platform on JAVA, Python,Node JS, AI, BlockChain, React, Angular, IOS, Android PHP,, Dot Net and C# technologies reflecting the vast exposure TSS has created for itself as well as keeping in line with the latest technologies.


Our Mission

Shriyash Software Solution Pvt. Ltd. has a policy of inclusive growth of the company which can be achieved only when each and every staff member of TSS has its growth in his/ her field and capacity. Once the staff member adds value to itself through the work done in TSS, then that adds the value to TSS. Satisfied and value added staff of TSS will promise successful product and application implementation at TSS’s clients which in turn is to the advantage of TSS’s client. This will provide an expanded customer base of Iterate which in turn will provide a stronger base for the members of Iterate.


Our Vision

Shriyash Software Solution Private Limited is committed to Customer Delight through extensive understanding of customer requirements and translating it into a system product which would be achieved and much more of its current requirement along with maintaining excellent quality control standards and continual improvement for its quality management systems.
